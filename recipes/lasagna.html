Lasagna Recipe

This is a delicious lasagna recipe that you can try at home!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Marinara sauce
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté garlic and onion until translucent.
  4. Add ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat mixture, ricotta, mozzarella, and marinara sauce. Repeat layers.
  6. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  7. Bake for 45 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  8. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Enjoy your homemade lasagna!
